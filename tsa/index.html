WHAT IS TSA?


TSA, or Technology Student Association, is a nationwide competition organization for students spanning a broad range of interests and passions through a diversity of events. It fosters a love of STEM among students, and provides an environment where they can incorporate their individual skills into a modern context. Members can compete at local, state, and national conferences.

ABOUT OUR CHAPTER


The TSA chapter at Florida Virtual School is a very unique one, as compared to others. Most chapters are comprised of a group of students who all go to the same brick and mortar school.
However, our chapter is comprised of a group of students from all over the state of Florida, since FLVS is a state-wide online school. The chapter was founded during the 2021-2022 school
year and hopes to succeed significantly in the coming years as it continues to grow.



Chapter Meetings


Since we all don't go to school with each other in-person and are spread out across the state, we conduct our meetings over Zoom. Every Monday night at 5:30pm, we go over upcoming
events and general announcements. This is also a good time for those who need to meet with their teams, who may not be able to throughout the week, in breakout
rooms to go over their projects.






CONFERENCES



FL STATE CONFERENCE


This is where everyone's hard work on their projects becomes worth it. During this conference, across 4 days, the entire state of Florida comes together to compete against each other
to see who's project is the best in the state for each event. The state conference for FL is held in Orlando, Florida.


NATIONAL CONFERENCE


Think States is something extravagant? Wait until you go to Nationals, where the entire country comes together! It gets even tougher than States, but it is an amazing experience to be a part of. It is held across 5 days, this year being located in Dallas, Texas. In years past it has been held in places such as National Harbor, Orlando, Atlanta, and even more places around the country!
